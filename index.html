Arriving & Getting Around

Most guests arrive via Genoa or Milan. From either, the countryside around Gavi is easily reachable by car. If you plan to explore beyond the immediate area, renting a car is strongly recommended.

Wine Country & Countryside

Staying near Gavi means vineyards, quiet villages, and long lunches. Visit a local winery for Gavi tastings, enjoy relaxed afternoons by the pool, and explore nearby towns for traditional Piedmontese food and markets. Life here moves at a gentler pace ‚Äî perfect for unwinding.

The Ligurian Coast

Within a short drive, you‚Äôll reach the Ligurian coastline. Spend a day swimming, walking along the water, and enjoying fresh seafood. For a more glamorous stop, visit Portofino, known for its harbour, boutiques, and aperitivo by the sea. If time allows, the villages of Cinque Terre offer breathtaking views, coastal trails, and colourful cliffside towns.

Adding a City Break

If you‚Äôre flying in or out of Milan, consider spending a night or two there for culture, shopping, and world-class dining. Genoa, closer to the venue, offers historic streets, excellent food, and a more local, lived-in feel.
